Dampened & Driven Harmonic Oscillator

This interactive tool visualizes the phase space behavior of a dampened and driven harmonic oscillator.

Parameters:

  • γ (gamma) - Damping coefficient (0.01 to 2)
  • ω (omega) - Driving frequency (0.1 to 5)
  • F₀ - Driving force amplitude (0 to 5)
  • k - Spring constant (0 to 5)
  • m - Mass (0.1 to 5)

Use the sliders below to adjust parameters and observe how the phase space trajectory changes in real-time.
